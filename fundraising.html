Other Ways of Fundraising

There are other ways of fundraising which you can use, none of which cost you any extra money.

easysearch.org.uk

A simple way of fundraising is to use easysearch as your search engine instead of Google, for example. You should get very similar results, and each search raises money for the school - a percentage of the advertising money is given to the registered good causes. Each individual search raises very little, but it all adds up!

Try easysearch now »

easyfundraising.org.uk

easyfundraising.org.uk offers another really simple way of raising funds for the school.
You need to create an account with the Challoner's Girls Foundation registered as your good cause. Then, if you decide to make an online purchase from a retailer who is involved in the scheme, visit easyfundraising.org.uk first and use their link to the retailer's website. The retailer then gives a percentage donation to the school, and your purchase costs exactly the same as it would have done otherwise.
Currently, almost 3000 retailers are involved, including John Lewis, Tesco, Sainsbury's, Argos and Ebay.

Sign up to easyfundraising.org.uk »

Amazon link

Similarly to easyfundraising.org.uk, visiting Amazon via a designated link before you start your shop will ensure that Amazon gives a percentage of your purchases as a donation to the school.
